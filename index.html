New Believer FAQ

How do I pray?

Talk to God honestly like a child to a loving Father. Use the ACTS pattern: Adoration, Confession, Thanksgiving, Supplication.

Which Bible should I read?

Start with John or Mark. Use ESV or NLT in English, or Tagalog versions (ASD, TLAB) for Filipino.
