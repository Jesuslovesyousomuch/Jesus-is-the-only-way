How to Do Devotionals

Three simple frameworks to help you meet with Jesus daily.

SOAP Method

  • S – Scripture: Write down the verse(s) you are reading.
  • O – Observation: Note what stands out—keywords, context, who’s speaking.
  • A – Application: How does it apply to your life today?
  • P – Prayer: Write a personal prayer based on the lesson.

📖 Example: Philippians 4:6–7 → Not to worry but pray. I need to trust God with my anxieties today.

Read more Watch videos

ACTS Method

  • A – Adoration: Praise God for who He is.
  • C – Confession: Write honestly the sins/struggles you need to bring to Him.
  • T – Thanksgiving: Note what you’re grateful for.
  • S – Supplication: Ask God for help—for yourself and others.

📖 Example: “Lord, You are my peace. Forgive my impatience. Thank You for guiding me yesterday. Please help me forgive others today.”

Read more Watch videos

HEAR Method

  • H – Highlight: Choose and highlight the key verse.
  • E – Explain: Write what the verse means in context.
  • A – Apply: How does it impact your daily walk?
  • R – Respond: Write a personal response or prayer.

📖 Example: John 15:5 → Apart from Jesus I can do nothing. I must depend on Him in my work today.
